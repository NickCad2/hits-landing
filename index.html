FOR FANS

Free Forever
01
Open & Discover

See every gig happening near you — sorted by time, closest first. Filter by Tonight, This Weekend, or Next 7 Days. No account required to browse.

02
Follow Your Favorites

Follow artists you love. Then flip on the Following filter in the Discover feed to see only their upcoming shows — your personal concert calendar, built as you go.

03
Book a Private Gig

Need live music for a wedding, party, or private event? Tap any artist's profile and send them a direct inquiry. Connect and negotiate in-app — no middleman.

FOR ARTISTS

$9.99 / mo
01
Build Your Profile

Set your genre tags, bio, and social links. Add availability badges — Private Events or Open to Bookings — so fans know you're reachable. Add your Venmo to accept tips directly.

02
Post Your Gig in Seconds

Venue, date, time, genre tags. Hit Post Gig. Your show is live instantly on the Discover feed and map — visible to every fan browsing near that venue.

03

Track followers, profile views, and gig views in your My Reach dashboard. Receive private gig inquiries directly in-app — weddings, parties, events. Every show is a chance to grow.

Discover Feed

ONE SCREEN.
EVERY SHOW.

The Discover feed is the heart of HITS. Every gig posted by every artist in the area shows up here — sorted by time, closest first. No algorithms. No noise. Just shows.

  • →Sorted by time — soonest shows at the top, always
  • →Distance on every card — know exactly how far the venue is
  • →Genre tags for instant filtering by your sound
  • →Following filter — one tap to see only artists you follow
  • →Posted directly by the artist — real info, every time

Map View

SEE EVERYTHING
ON THE MAP.

Every gig on the Discover feed also appears as a pin on the map. Zoom out and see what's happening across the whole city. Then tap a pin to get the details.

  • →Pins mark every live gig near you at a glance
  • →Search any city or zip code to plan ahead
  • →Filter by date or genre — same controls as the feed
  • →Tap a pin for full gig details without leaving the map

Artist Tools

YOUR PROFILE.
YOUR BRAND.

Your HITS profile is more than a gig list. Signal your availability, collect tips, and track exactly who's watching — all from one dashboard built for working musicians.

  • →Availability badges — display "Private Events" or "Open to Bookings" so fans know you're reachable
  • →Venmo tip button — fans can tip you directly from your profile, no third-party app required
  • →My Reach analytics — see your total followers, profile views, and gig views at a glance
  • →Per-gig view & save counts — know which shows are getting traction before night-of
  • →Inquiries inbox — all private gig requests in one place, ready to accept or decline

Private Gigs

FANS FIND YOU.
YOU GET BOOKED.

Fans planning a wedding, party, or private event can find you on HITS and reach out directly. No agents, no booking fees, no commission — just a real conversation between you and a potential client.

  • →For fans — tap "Send Inquiry" on any artist profile to pitch your event
  • →For artists — inquiries land in your Inquiries inbox with full event details
  • →Accept or decline — you're always in control of who you respond to
  • →In-app messaging — once accepted, negotiate and confirm everything inside HITS
  • →Zero commission — whatever rate you agree on is yours entirely
